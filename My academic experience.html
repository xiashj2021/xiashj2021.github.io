my senior high school life

Undeniably, because of the college entrance examination, high school life is very nervous and academic pressure is very big. I have consistently maintained a keen interest in mathematics and physics and have often done well in physics examinations. We implement the mode of arts and science subject to take an examination. Before the subject is divided, my history score is also very good. I often read some extracurricular books about history and have a strong interest in it. We classified geography as a liberal arts subject in high school, so I didn't have enough opportunities to study it deeply after the division. But it still attracted me deeply, because nothing is more exciting than sailing and discovering the unknown.

my experience of computer science

My parents are both teachers, so I have the opportunity to have access to the computer when I was very young. Because my parents like to use slides in class, I learned how to make slides first. When I was in primary school, I liked to design cool custom animations to show the contents of slides, which I thought was very interesting. In the days that followed, I basically understood every program in the computer. Basically, problems with the computer operating system and the use of office software will not trouble me. It is a pity that I haven't systematically studied further knowledge about developing programs and designing websites before college. Junior high school and senior high school teachers do not attach importance to computer courses because of the entrance examination and the computer courses are not within the scope of the examination. At the same time we also don't have enough time to learn computer knowledge. I think such a situation will gradually get better in the future.

my university life

I am very glad that our school is a comprehensive university. I can communicate with teachers and students who study different fields. In view of the previous unilateral study of science knowledge, I think it is time to improve my literary quality. I will read more literature books during my university period to make up for my lack of spiritual world. At the same time, in the high school stage, theoretical knowledge is relatively more learned, which does not provide us with a platform for practical exercise. As a member of the Information College, I will work harder to master more information knowledge and improve my practical ability.
